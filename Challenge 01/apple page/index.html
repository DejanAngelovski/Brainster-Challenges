iPad


iPad is a line of tablet computers designed, developed and marketed by Apple Inc., which run the IOS mobile operating system. The first iPad was released on April 3, 2010; the most recent iPad models are ipad (2018), released on March 27, 2018, the 10.5-inch (270mm) and 12.9-inch (330mm 2G iPad Pro released on June 13, 2017. The user interface is build around the device's multi-touch screen, including a virtual keyboard. All iPads can connect via Wi-Fi; some models also have cellular connectivity.

MacBookAir


The MacBook Air is a line of Macintosh subnotebook computers developed and manufactured by Apple Inc. It consists of a full-size keyboard, a machined aluminum case, and a thin light structure. The Air is available with a screen size of (measured diagonally) 13.3in (33.782 cm), with different specifications produced by Apple. Since 2011, all MacBook Air models have used solid-state drive storage and intel Core i5 or i7 CPUs.
